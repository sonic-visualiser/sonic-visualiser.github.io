Don't forget your plugins!

  • Analysis plugins: Oh yes, you'll want some of those. Go to the Vamp plugin download page.
    No plugins are included with Sonic Visualiser itself.

  • Effects plugins: Sonic Visualiser can use LADSPA and DSSI effects plugins. These are Linux-native formats, though Sonic Visualiser can use them on any platform if you can find the plugins.

  • VST, AudioUnit, LV2? Sonic Visualiser does not currently support any of these formats — sorry.
